About Us

Our clinician has been a nurse for 30 years which includes over 20 years’ experience as an advanced nurse practitioner. Along with a lifetime interest in health, well being and nutrition she possesses a master’s degree in clinical practice, a non-medical prescribing qualification and has undertaken extensive training in weight management via the National Medical Weight Loss Program. This places her is in a unique position to help you with your
journey.

After consultation you may be prescribed the latest in weight loss medication with scientifically proven results.

Psychotherapist

Low mood and stress can raise cortisol levels which can in turn increase appetite and cravings.

Therefore as an add on to the service we are delighted to have partnered with a highly specialised practitioner. We want you to achieve lasting results by concentrating on the psychological aspects as well as the physical of weight health management.

Nutritionist

Nutritionist services provide expert guidance on healthy eating habits, dietary planning, and nutritional support tailored to individual needs. Whether you're aiming to manage a medical condition, improve athletic performance, lose weight, or simply adopt a healthier lifestyle, a qualified nutritionist can create personalized meal plans and offer practical advice based on your goals and preferences. These services often include one-on-one consultations, ongoing support, and education to help clients make informed food choices, improve overall well-being, and sustain long-term results.
